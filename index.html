🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  SafeRide is a personal cycling safety tool. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, where it lives, and what we do with it.

What We Collect

When you create a profile we collect:

  • Full name — to personalise your experience
  • Email address — to identify your profile on this device
  • Cyclist handle — displayed when you share community routes

Where Your Data Lives

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your own device. SafeRide has no servers, no databases, and no cloud accounts. Your information never leaves your device.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not send your data to any server or third party
  • We do not send marketing, promotional, or any other emails
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information
  • We do not use tracking pixels, cookies, or analytics
  • We do not create accounts on external platforms on your behalf

Location Data

Your GPS location is used only within the current session to centre the map and find nearby bike paths. It is not stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere. You can deny location access at any time in your device settings.

Dog Hazards & Saved Routes

Pins you place and routes you save are stored locally on your device only. They are visible only to you unless you explicitly choose to share a route via the Community Routes feature, in which case the route name, handle, distance, and notes are saved locally and displayed to other users of the same device.

Third-Party Map Services

To display maps and calculate routes, the app contacts these free open-source services. They receive only the minimum location data needed to function:

  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles
  • OSRM — bicycle route calculation
  • Nominatim — address search
  • Overpass API — bike path data

Each service has its own privacy policy. None of them receive your name or email.

Deleting Your Data

To delete all your SafeRide data: open your browser or Safari settings, navigate to Website Data or Storage, and clear data for this site. This permanently removes your profile, saved routes, and all hazard pins.

Children's Privacy

SafeRide is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please ask a parent or guardian before creating a profile.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated date at the top will reflect it. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? You can reach the developer through the app's community or support channels.
